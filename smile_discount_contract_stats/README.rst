This module depends on ``smile_discount_contract`` module ,it adds business intelligence reports for discount contracts and
``next slice`` field for Discount contract lines tree view .

Usage
=====

To display discount contracts analysis :

#. Go to ``Accounting > Reports > Business Intelligence >Discounts`` menu .
